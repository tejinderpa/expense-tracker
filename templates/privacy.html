Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (email address, password)
  • Expense data (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions)
  • Any other information you choose to enter into the app

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Spendly service
  • Display your expense history and analytics
  • Improve and optimize the application
  • Communicate with you about updates or issues

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You retain ownership of your data. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may use trusted third-party service providers to assist us in operating our application, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses minimal cookies and local storage only for essential functionality, such as maintaining your session. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
